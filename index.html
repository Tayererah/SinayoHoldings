Our Story

PROFESSIONAL CONSTRUCTION.
PROVEN RESULTS.

Sinayo Holdings is a Zimbabwe-based construction company headquartered in Bulawayo. We pride ourselves on delivering excellence across every project — from intimate residential builds to large-scale commercial and civil infrastructure.

Our team of qualified engineers, architects and project managers combine local expertise with international standards to ensure every build exceeds client expectations.

  • Licensed and registered construction company in Zimbabwe
  • Full project lifecycle management — from blueprint to handover
  • Committed to quality materials, safety standards & timely delivery
  • Trusted by commercial clients, developers and homeowners alike
